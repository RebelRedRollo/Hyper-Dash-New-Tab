To edit the preset bookmarks, or delete them altogether, click on the Edit button on your New Tab.

To add your own, click on the Add button.

To use the built-in search bar, click on it, input your query, and press Enter.

You can change its search engine in Settings (cog) > Header > Search.

Speaking of the Settings area, nightTab (and therefore Hyper Dash New Tab) offers absolutely tons of customization options.

Tweak at will!

Thanks for using Hyper Dash New Tab!
